Gear Watch Designer

  • BETA DOWNLOAD for Win Download
  • BETA DOWNLOAD for Mac Download

Design, produce, and distribute your own Gear watch faces without the complexity of code scripting.

Now in beta,

Anybody who is interested to make Gear watch faces can join this beta program.
Help us perfect Gear Watch Designer by giving us feedback about this tool’s stability or any performance issues you may have encountered

Now in beta,

Quick. Easy. Fun.

Create your design elements, assemble them, then upload your Gear watch faces directly to the store.

No Experience Needed

The interface has been designed to be immediately familiar to anyone who’s used to working with applications like Adobe Photoshop and Flash.

Unleash your creativity

Packed with powerful features to bring your ideas to life

  • Conditional line based component control

    Adjust the conditional line of each component for maximum flexibility and customization.
    For example, you can show the SUNNY background image during the day (06:00 ~ 17:59), then show the MOON background image at night (18:00 ~ 05:59). You can also apply conditions based on battery level and number of steps taken.

  • Get animated

    Use PNG sequences to create dynamic and engaging animations for your Gear watch faces.

Requirements

  • Microsoft Windows® 7 or later (32- or 64-bit), Apple Mac OS® X 10.8(Mountain Lion) 64-bit or later

  • At least dual-core 2 GHz of CPU

  • At least 2 GB of RAM memory

  • At least 1 GB of free disk space

  • JRE (Java Runtime Environment) 1.8 or later.

  • 1280x1024 resolution (higher resolution recommended)
