Designing apps for Samsung Gear

In this section, you will learn about basic design principles and concept of Samsung Gear. Follow these guidelines and references to make your Gear app enjoyable.

Note

The Samsung Gear S2 design is based on the wearable design principles of TIZEN.
For more Information on TIZEN wearable designs, please refer to the design guide available at developer.tizen.org/design/wearable
